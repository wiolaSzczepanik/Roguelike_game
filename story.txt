 The game shows the journey of beginner programmer. Every bug
 found on our way is a mortal enemy which we must destroy. We,
 who set the first steps in this world have to go this journey 
 alone. Each defeated problem helps us to become a better 
 programmer. We can count on a more experienced programmer, 
 but we must deserve it. 

 Come... take this trip with us.
